This website lists some fun things that you can do in animal crossing. Click the sign up to get newsletters every week where I explore the game and send out some tips and tricks.

Things To Do

Fishing is a fun activity in ACNH. Tons of games have a fishing feature, and it's a fun activity in nearly all of them.

Visit Harv''s Island. There are so many fun things to do there, you'll never run out of fun things to do!

Talk to visitors. Visitors sometimes sell exclusive items so you may find something interesting.

Dig up fossils. You can donate them to the museum or sell them for a pretty penny.
